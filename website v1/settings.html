Privacy Settings

Advertising

Manage your ad experience on Doorble.

Allow personalized advertising
When selected, you agree to some of your activity (e.g. pages you’ve liked, information on your Profile, ads you’ve clicked on, places you’ve told us you visited) on Doorble to help us show you ads that we think you might be interested in. Learn more about how ads are personalized to you.


Location Data

Manage your location data on Doorble.

Share Location data
When selected, you agree to share your location with Doorble. We use location-related information only if you turn on the Location data-toggle. This includes things like your GPS location and, depending on the operating system you're using, other device signals. Learn more about our location data practices.


Audience & Tagging

Manage what information you allow other people on Doorble to see.

Protect your posts
When selected, your posts and other account information are only visible to people who follow you.

Photo tagging
Allow people to tag you in their photos and receive notifications when they do so.


Discoverability & Contacts

Decide whether people who have your email address or phone number can find and connect with you on Doorble.

Let people who have your email address find you on Doorble
Let people who have your email address find and connect with you on Doorble.

Let people who have your phone number find you on Doorble
Let people who have your phone number find and connect with you on Doorble.
